About the Project

The Story Behind AIR NOVA

A student-led initiative to solve the defining urban challenge of our generation — combining AI, vertical farming, and clean energy into one cohesive system.

Our Mission

Why We Built This

Urban air pollution kills millions every year. Cities struggle to feed their populations sustainably. Existing solutions are fragmented, expensive, and often ineffective. We believed there had to be a better way.

AIR NOVA started as an idea — what if we could monitor air quality, purify it, grow local food, and power it all with solar energy, all in one integrated urban system?

Three students with skills in AI, design, and hardware came together to make it real. The result is AIR NOVA — a modular, AI-driven urban platform that cities can deploy today.

Mission
Clean Air for Everyone

Every person deserves to breathe clean air. We make that possible through technology and design.

Vision

Cities that grow their own food, clean their own air, and power themselves sustainably.

Module 01 · AI + Agriculture

Green Zone

An AI-controlled greenhouse that automatically monitors, adjusts, and optimizes crop growth year-round — with zero pesticides.

How It Works

Farming, Reimagined

Green Zone uses machine learning to continuously analyze crop data from an array of sensors. It automatically adjusts irrigation, LED spectrum and intensity, nutrient dosing, and temperature — all in real time, with no human intervention needed.

🧠
ML Crop Monitoring

Computer vision and sensor fusion detect early signs of stress, disease, or nutrient deficiency before they become problems.

💧
Precision Irrigation

Hydroponic delivery systems give each plant exactly what it needs — reducing water use by up to 90% vs. traditional farming.

💡
Adaptive LED Lighting

Spectrum-tunable LEDs adjust for each growth stage — seedling, vegetative, flowering — maximizing photosynthesis efficiency.

🌡️
Climate Control

Automated HVAC maintains ideal temperature, humidity, and CO₂ concentration for continuous high-yield growth.

☀️
Solar Powered

Every watt is sourced from on-site solar panels. Green Zone produces food with zero grid energy consumption.

Module 02 · Air Purification

Serenity Tower

A vertical air purification system that actively scrubs CO₂ and pollutants from urban air, returning clean oxygen to the city.

The Technology

Urban Air, Restored

Serenity Tower draws in polluted urban air through multi-stage filtration. Photocatalytic oxidation, HEPA filtration, and activated carbon layers work in concert to remove particulates, VOCs, CO₂, and biological contaminants.

🔬
Multi-Stage Filtration

HEPA + activated carbon + photocatalytic layers remove PM2.5, PM10, VOCs, NOx, and biological particles.

🌬️
Continuous Operation

Processes thousands of cubic meters of air per hour — quietly and continuously, day and night.

📡
Air Quality Sensing

Real-time sensor arrays detect pollution spikes and increase purification intensity automatically.

🌿
Biophilic Design

Living wall integration enhances natural air scrubbing while creating green visual anchors in urban spaces.

🔋
Energy Recovery

Heat exchangers recover thermal energy from exhaust, feeding it back into the system for near-zero net energy use.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about AIR NOVA and our approach to urban sustainability.

What is AIR NOVA?
+
AIR NOVA is an AI-driven urban platform combining air purification, vertical farming, and solar energy to make cities healthier, more sustainable, and self-sufficient. It consists of three integrated modules: Green Zone, Serenity Tower, and Smart Dashboard.
How does Green Zone work?
+
Green Zone uses machine learning and sensor fusion to continuously monitor crops in a controlled vertical farm environment. It automatically adjusts water delivery, LED lighting spectrum, and nutrient levels for optimal growth year-round — with no pesticides and 90% less water than conventional farming.
What does Serenity Tower actually remove from the air?
+
Serenity Tower uses multi-stage filtration — HEPA, activated carbon, and photocatalytic oxidation layers — to remove PM2.5, PM10, volatile organic compounds (VOCs), nitrogen oxides (NOx), CO₂, and biological contaminants including bacteria and mold spores.
Is AIR NOVA entirely solar powered?
+
Yes. All AIR NOVA modules are designed to operate entirely on on-site solar generation. Excess energy is stored in battery systems for overnight and low-light operation. Energy recovery systems within Serenity Tower further reduce net consumption.
Who built AIR NOVA?
+
AIR NOVA was created by three students: Pranav (software and AI), Aawarth (design and UI), and Amog (hardware and robotics). Each member brings deep specialist knowledge, and together they cover every layer of the AIR NOVA system.
Can AIR NOVA be deployed in any city?
+
Yes. AIR NOVA is designed to be modular and scalable. Individual modules can be deployed independently or as a complete integrated system. The platform adapts to varying urban densities, climates, and local regulations — from small districts to city-wide networks.
How does the Smart Dashboard help city planners?
+
The Smart Dashboard aggregates real-time data from all AIR NOVA modules and presents it in a single intuitive interface. City planners gain live air quality maps, food production metrics, energy analytics, predictive maintenance alerts, and sustainability scoring — enabling data-driven urban policy decisions.
